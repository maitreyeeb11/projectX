Vocational Training Support

Child: Kavya, 16 years old

Success Story
Kavya, a 16-year-old girl, had a strong passion for tailoring and dreamed of turning it into a sustainable career. However, she lacked the financial resources to enroll in a vocational training course or purchase the necessary sewing equipment to develop her skills. Despite her determination, Kavya’s financial situation made it difficult for her to access the training she needed.
Udaan’s platform recognized Kavya’s potential and showcased her story on the Sponsorship Portal. The platform shared Kavya’s passion for tailoring and her goal of becoming financially independent. As a result, several generous donors contributed to funding her vocational training and sewing equipment.

Outcome: With the help of the funds raised through Udaan’s platform, Kavya successfully completed her tailoring training and invested in the equipment required to start her own business. Today, Kavya runs a small tailoring business, providing for her family and saving for her future goals. She is not only building a stable career but also becoming an inspiration to others in her community who face similar financial challenges.
