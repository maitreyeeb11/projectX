Mentor Guidelines

Welcome to Udaan! As a mentor, you play a vital role in shaping the future of our mentees. Please review the following guidelines to ensure a positive and impactful mentorship experience.

Role and Expectations

  • Provide guidance and share your expertise to help mentees grow academically and personally.
  • Maintain regular communication and offer constructive feedback.
  • Encourage mentees to set goals and work towards achieving them.

Code of Conduct

  • Maintain professionalism and respect in all interactions.
  • Respect the privacy and confidentiality of your mentees.
  • Avoid any form of discrimination, harassment, or inappropriate behavior.

Mentorship Best Practices

  • Listen actively and show empathy towards your mentees’ concerns.
  • Share resources and opportunities that can aid in their development.
  • Be patient and flexible, adapting to your mentees’ unique needs.

Contact and Support

If you need assistance or have any concerns, please contact us at mentor@udaan.
