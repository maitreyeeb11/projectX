Volunteer Guidelines

Welcome to Udaan! We are thrilled to have you as part of our team. Please review the following guidelines to ensure a successful and meaningful volunteer experience.

Code of Conduct

  • Maintain respect and professionalism in all interactions.
  • Be committed to the tasks you accept and inform us promptly if unable to fulfill them.
  • Always represent Udaan positively and uphold its values.

Volunteer Responsibilities

  • Complete your assignments responsibly and on time.
  • Adhere to all instructions provided by your team leader or supervisor.
  • Keep all sensitive information confidential.

Health and Safety

  • Follow all safety instructions during events or field activities.
  • Report any incidents or issues immediately to the coordinator.

Contact and Support

If you need assistance or have any concerns, please contact us at volunteer@udaan.
