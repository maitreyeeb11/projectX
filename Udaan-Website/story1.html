Building Confidence Through Art

Child: Meena, 15 years old

Success Story
Priya, a dedicated volunteer and trained art therapist, identified Meena's potential and reached out through Udaan’s platform to offer support. Every week, Priya conducted online art therapy sessions, tailored specifically to Meena's needs.
These sessions provided Meena with a safe space to express her feelings and emotions, explore creativity, and articulate her experiences through art. Priya encouraged Meena to use painting as a form of self-expression, guiding her in understanding and channeling her emotions into colorful, meaningful artwork.

Outcome: Over time, Meena's confidence began to grow. Her artwork, rich with themes of hope, resilience, and struggle, started to be featured at community events organized through Udaan. These public displays of Meena’s creativity allowed her to connect with others, share her story, and gain recognition for her talent. Her work was eventually showcased in a fundraising exhibition, which not only boosted her self-esteem but also generated funds to support her future through vocational training. Today, Meena dreams of becoming financially independent and using her art to continue spreading positivity and hope within her community.
