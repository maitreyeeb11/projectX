Career Workshops and Volunteership

Child: Rahul, 16 years old

Success Story
Rahul, a 16-year-old boy from a marginalized background, felt lost when it came to choosing a career. Without proper guidance, he struggled to identify his strengths and interests. The lack of mentorship made it difficult for Rahul to make informed decisions about his future, and he was unsure about the steps to take in order to build a successful career.
Through Udaanâ€™s platform, Amit, a volunteer career coach, connected with Rahul. Amit organized a virtual career workshop, where Rahul and other participants learned about various career paths, the skills required for each, and how to craft an impactful resume. Rahul discovered his interest in web development during the workshop, which sparked his enthusiasm for the tech field.

Outcome: Motivated by the workshop, Rahul enrolled in an online web development course, sponsored by Udaan, to gain the necessary skills for a career in technology. Rahul is now in the process of building his first website for a local NGO, applying the web development skills he learned. His journey has just begun, but Rahul is excited about the future and confident in his ability to create a meaningful career.
